Yoga Asanas






1.Tadasana [Mountain Pose]

Stand tall with your feet together, arms at your sides. Tadasana helps in grounding and improving posture, which can boost self-confidence and reduce stress.

2.Uttanasana [Standing Forward Bend]

Bend forward from the waist while keeping your legs straight. This asana relieves stress, calms the mind, and helps with anxiety.

3.Balasana [Child's Pose]

Kneel down, sit back on your heels, and stretch your arms forward on the floor. It's a calming pose that can release tension and ease anxiety.

4.Paschimottanasana [Seated Forward Bend]

Sit with your legs straight and bend forward to touch your toes. This asana is effective in calming the mind and relieving stress.

5.Savasana [Corpse Pose]

Lie flat on your back with arms and legs relaxed. Savasana is a powerful relaxation pose that helps in reducing stress and promoting mental calmness.

6.Halasana [Plow Pose]

Lie on your back and lift your legs over your head until they touch the floor behind you. Halasana can help alleviate stress and improve focus and concentration.

7. Setu Bandhasana [Bridge Pose]

Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat on the floor. Lift your hips while keeping your feet and shoulders on the ground. This pose can relieve anxiety and mild depression.

8. Viparita Karani [Legs Up the Wall Pose]

Lie on your back with your legs extended up against a wall. This pose can calm the mind, reduce anxiety, and improve circulation.

9.Bhujangasana [Cobra Pose]

Lie face down and lift your upper body using your arms, keeping the lower body on the ground. It can help reduce stress, anxiety, and depression.

10.Anjaneyasana [Low Lunge Pose]

Step one foot forward into a lunge position and stretch your arms overhead. This asana can help release tension and promote a sense of balance.
